WEATHER

Rain Season
Very Mild Temperatures
Very Sunny

Typically, in Southern California, summers are warm and dry while winter are damp and mild. Rain season begins in October - November usually very light. December, January, and February are the heaviest rain months. Then season usually ends March - April which tends to be very light as well. On average Southern California will see 300 Sunny days a year. While average high temperatures in San Diego, my favorite city of all time, will run mid to upper 60's during the winter and mid to upper 70's during the summer.
